Features:
* 100% working implementation of the Pantheon likelihood in Mathematica. 
* Marginalization over M like in Appendix C of Ref. [1].
* Reproduces results from Ref. [2].
* Data and other stuff can be found in Ref. [3].
* New in v1.2: Possibility to test subsets of the full compilation.
* New in v1.1: Updated heliocentric redshifts given by lcparam_full_long_zhel.txt given in Ref. [4].

References:
[1] Conley, arXiv: 1104.1443
[2] Scolnic et al, arXiv: 1710.00845
[3] https://archive.stsci.edu/prepds/ps1cosmo/index.html
[4] https://github.com/dscolnic/Pantheon
